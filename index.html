FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Bentzen offer?

Bentzen offers a comprehensive suite of digital services including App Development (iOS & Android), Website Development, Custom Software Development, Facebook Ads, Google Ads, Instagram Ads, WhatsApp Automation, and UGC Ad production. We provide end-to-end digital solutions tailored to your business needs.

How long does it take to build a website or app?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A standard business website typically takes 2–4 weeks. A custom web platform or mobile app can take 4–12 weeks. We provide detailed project timelines during the discovery phase and maintain transparent communication throughout the development process.

What is the cost of your services?

Our pricing is project-based and depends on scope, complexity, and requirements. We offer competitive pricing while maintaining premium quality. Please contact us via our inquiry form or WhatsApp to receive a customized quote for your project.

Do you work with businesses outside of Ahmedabad?

Absolutely! While we are headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we work with clients across India and internationally. Our collaborative, remote-friendly workflow ensures seamless communication and project delivery regardless of geography.

Will I get a dedicated project manager?

Yes. Every client at Bentzen gets a dedicated project manager who serves as your single point of contact, ensuring clear communication, timely updates, and smooth project execution from kick-off to launch.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support and maintenance packages. These include bug fixes, performance optimization, security updates, feature additions, and 24/7 technical support. We are long-term partners in your digital journey.

What industries do you specialize in?

Bentzen has delivered projects across e-commerce, real estate, education, healthcare, industrial, hospitality, and professional services. Our adaptable approach allows us to understand domain nuances and deliver contextually relevant digital solutions.

How do I get started with Bentzen?

Simply reach out via our Contact section, WhatsApp, or email. We'll schedule a free discovery call to understand your goals, after which we provide a detailed proposal and timeline. The process is quick, transparent, and commitment-free at the initial stage.
